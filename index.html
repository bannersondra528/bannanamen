About Us

Studio Five Spaces is a boutique interior design studio rooted in the belief that a well-designed space can shape the way we live, feel, and connect. With a passion for storytelling and a commitment to craftsmanship, we design environments that are both aesthetically grounded and deeply personal.

Founded by creatives with backgrounds in architecture, art direction, and interior styling, our studio thrives at the intersection of form and feeling. Every detail — from layout to lighting — is thoughtfully considered to serve a greater narrative.

Our Services

We offer a range of design services tailored to residential, commercial, and boutique hospitality spaces. Whether you're refreshing a single room or launching a new property, we’re here to translate your vision into a compelling and cohesive interior experience.

Our core offerings include:

  • Full-service interior design and spatial planning
  • Furniture and material sourcing
  • Custom millwork and cabinetry design
  • Art curation and styling
  • On-site consulting and project coordination

Our Approach

We approach each project with curiosity, intention, and respect for the space’s history and future. No two spaces — and no two clients — are the same, which is why our work is never formulaic. We begin by listening: to your story, your goals, and your lifestyle.

Through collaboration and careful refinement, we craft environments that feel lived-in, layered, and enduring. Our process is transparent and grounded, ensuring that every choice — from big-picture concepts to finishing touches — aligns with your needs and the soul of the space.
